### Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex
- Multiple Reactions:
	- Decarboxylation Reaction
	- Transacetylase Reaction
	- Dehydrogenase Reaction
- Irreversible process
	- CO2 is produced, which is immediately converted into bicarbonate
	- Exergonic

### Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Regulation
- Is Allosterically Regulated
	- Negative feedback inhibition by Acetyl-CoA
- Is inhibited by a high ratio of NADH/NAD+ (mean not enough NAD+ to recieve electron from FADH2)
- Is regulated by reversible Phosphorylation of the E1 subunit
	- PDKs = Pyruvate dehydrogenase kinases - Inactivated
	- PDPs = Pyruvate dehydrogenase phosphatases - Activated
