### What happens after DNA is cut
Cells have two main mechanisms to repair a double-stranded DNA break
#### Non-homologous end joining (NHEJ): 
- Repaired by random nucleotide insertions: can repair or disrupt a gene depending upon the insertion.
- Many proteins are working on it
- Random mutations -> frameshift  -> Knockout gene very easily 
#### Homology-directed repair (HDR):
- More precise repair through an exact template match. A cell can be fooled to repair using introduced DNA and thus change the genetic code in a predictable manner. (Too good to be true?)
- Not very efficient in practice
