### CRISPR practice
5'-AAAAAAAAACCCCCGGGGGAAAAATTTTTCGGAAAAAAAAA-3'
3'-TTTTTTTTT GGGGGCCCCCTTTTT AAAAAGCCTTTTTTTTT-5'

1. What is the PAM for this spacer below in the target DNA sequence above?
   "CCCCCGGGGGAAAAATTTTT" 
   A: CGG
2. Which strand is the Cas9 going to bind with the above spacer? (Upper strand or lower strand)
   A: Target Strand = Lower strand
3. Where is the Cas9 going to cut
   A: ...TTT|TTTCGG... (three residues before PAM)

Cas9 looks for PAM first, and then matches the space. If PAM matches but doesn't match with the spacer, it doesn't do anything?
