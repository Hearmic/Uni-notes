### Base editing APOE4 to APOE3r
- The apolipoprotein E gene has four isoforms that differ at amino acids 112 and 158
- APOE4 is the largest and most common genetic risk factor for late-onset Alzheimer's disease
- APOE mechanism of action is unclear.
- APOE4 can be converted to APOE3r by base editing
