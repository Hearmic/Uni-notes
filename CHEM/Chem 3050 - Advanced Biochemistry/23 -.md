### Small Chemical Modification
- S-Adenosylmethionine (SAM) (Methyl donor)
- DNA 
	- Cytosine
	- suppress gene expression

- Lys, Arg Methylation
- Histone Methylation (either activate or repress gene expression)


### Larger Scale Modification
- Ubiquitination
- Sumoylation
- Glycosylation
- Lipidation

Permanent alteration of structure
Routinely added & removed

#### Examples
- Ubiquitin (many Lysine)
	- Release DNA (Similar to acetylation)
	- Poly Ub -> Degradation
- SUMO
	- Attach to Lys
	- Nuclear. function (transcription)
