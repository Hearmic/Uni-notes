#### Serine Proteases
- Chymotrypsin $\leftrightarrow$ Subtillsin,  (convergent evolution)
- Chymotrypsin, Subtillsin, Elastase share similar function
- But diverge in protein substrates
- Recognize different R group
	- Trypsin: Asp side chain stabilized by Lys
	- Chymotrypsin: hydrophobic pocket (non-polar R group aromatic)
	- Elastase: smaller binding site (Only arrow alanine, due to bulky group in the pocket)
