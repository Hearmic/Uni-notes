### Type
1. Directional Selection
   Change the average value of traits
   Genetic variation $\to$ reduced
2. Stabilizing Selection
   No Change the average value of traits
   Genetic variation $\to$ reduced
3. Diversifying (Disruptive) Selection
   Genetic variation $\to$ maintained
4. Balancing selection
   Genetic variation $\to$ maintained
	- Fitness varies in time & space
	- Heterozygote advantage
	- ex) Sickle Cell Allele (heterozygotes do not suffer from both anemia and Malaria)
