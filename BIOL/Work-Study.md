### Requirements
- Have a valid study permit
- Have a valid social insurance number (SIN)
- Study permit conditions allow
- Full-time student (apart from final semester)
- No limit on the number of hours
- Work authorization ends once you complete your program, regardless of the expiry date on your study permit

Valid study permit: Must include the work authorization listed in the conditions on the permit.
Full-time Enrollment: 9 credits/term (without a disability) or 6 credits/term (with a disability)
